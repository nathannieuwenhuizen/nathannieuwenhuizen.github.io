Description

A game jam we made during "Mediabites 2016" where I volunteered with 7 other developers and artists to make a game with the theme "connecting".

In 24 hours we had to come up with a concept and make a prototype/game based around the concept.

The 24 hours were not as hard as I thought it would be, but in the last couple of hours we struggled to keep awake. Luckilly we finished our prototype: Crystales!.

It was a unique experience for me and my team and personally want to do it again next year!

My role

  • Programming the lacer
  • Time counter
  • Designing two levels.

    • Game features:

  • Multiple levels
  • Camera movement
  • Particles
  • Fireflies and lizards
  • Shining up the level with each crystal your connect.
  • Breaking the circuit if you make a loop with the light.
