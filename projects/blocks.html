Description

In the beginning of our second year at Media College Amsterdam, we were assigned to make a '2D endless runner' game with a team of mainly three developers.

It is made in a 3D Unity and we asked permission from the client to use it if we were able to look like 2D.

I have learned many things in this project and was my introduction to Unity.
