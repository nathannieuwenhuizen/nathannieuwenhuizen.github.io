Summary

A 8 week project for school where we made a game for mobile and set in for free in the Google play store.
Our subject for the game was mythe, so we chose the rainbow serpent (a fable animal) to be the theme of our game.
This is my first mobile game I've worked so far and with the biggest team so far (10 people). And I am proud of the result!s

My role

-Programming the "sky form" and transition into it.
-Proggramming the healthbar.
-Programming the enemies.
-Designing some of the level parts.

Game features:

-Endless runner
-Online highscore
-Static and moving obstacles
-Increasing difficulty
-Randomanized chunks
-Tutorial chunks for beginners
-Snake movement
-Particles for game feel
